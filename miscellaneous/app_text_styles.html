AppTextStyles Showcase

Below are four style “swatches” reflecting the same definitions from AppTextStyles in Flutter: displayLarge, titleMedium, bodyMedium, and bodySmall. We’re using the “Roboto” font from Google Fonts (weights 400, 600, 700). The background is dark to mimic a typical Material dark theme environment.

This is an example of displayLarge. Perfect for hero headlines or top-level titles.

This is titleMedium. Perfect for subheadings or mid-level headings.

bodyMedium

bodyMedium
